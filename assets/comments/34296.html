My interest (and, I admit, I did much of the replying thee) was simply this question:Given a starting population of 3.5 million “Holocaust survivors” in 1945 (what seems an official claim), can we really expect to have about 245,000 survivors (in 2024, also an “official” claim)?I’m just an amateur with actuarial reckoning.
