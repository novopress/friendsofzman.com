For every locality __A__ the term “the government of A” refers to the largest dealer in interpersonal violence in that locality (definition, after Weber). The size of a government is a function, in part, of the speed of communication and transportation. For a government to cohere, orders and troops must move from the center to the periphery in time to meet challenges. Most large empires were noted for their communication and transportation technology: Egypt for the Nile (lower the sail and drift downriver, raise the sail and sail upriver), Rome, the Inca, and the Americans for roads. Qin Shi Huangdi standardized axle lengths and built roads and canals specifically to supply his army.In terms of the speed of communication and transportation, the world today is smaller than Rome at its height or Han Dynasty China. National boundaries may persist for a while, like ancient mid-continental plate boundaries or flaws in a block of ice.Perhaps this answers the astrobiologists’ question: “Where are they?” Perhaps once a civilization dominates an entire planet the competitive spur to further advance in transportation technology wanes.
