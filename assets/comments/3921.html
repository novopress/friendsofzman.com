But, as playing away from home was not an indictable offence, the moment passed and gloom returned to the Crown.Justice was done from what we could see and my final view was the two families involved glaring at each other as lad left the court a free man.I don’t know if this one case was recorded as a near miss, a false allegation or more evidence of how terrible men are, but I am glad I saw it for what it was.
