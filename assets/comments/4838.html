‘Christianity’ is simply man’s attempt to live out the Bible. We often err in this endeavor for myriad reasons and much of Christendom is flawed (including but not limited to most Evangelical denominations), but I wouldn’t throw the baby out with the bath water. Humanity is inherently flawed and therefore our pursuits are prone to failure. However I would posit it is inadvisable to dismiss Christianity because its proclaimed followers/believers err.The Bible is self-contained and self-explanatory (at least for those major doctrines which concern our mortal life). It is the road map provided by the Creator who wishes to interact with his creation. Many flavors of Christianity have corrupted this with their interpretations, agendas, etc. However if we examine the core of what is contained in that text, we find the only rational and logical moral code which fills the spiritual void all humanity must satisfy. To deny the existence of Christ as God ultimately leads humanity to worship itself. To fill that spiritual void with any other deity leaves us unfulfilled. To know God exists requires interaction; ‘proof’ if you will. That is what the Bible explains to us; how to interact with our Creator.So I encourage you to not be disenfranchised in your faith by the failures of humanity to represent God. He needs no representatives. Ditch the intermediaries and find God directly through you’re own experience. There’s more to this life than what we see and can explain.Enjoy your posts and thought provoking essays.
