thezman #5910 July 27, 2015 2:43 pm 0
Most “free apps” rely on ads. They charge the advertiser based on “impressions” which loosely means the number of times the ad is viewed. They will track the click throughs which is supposed to mean the number of times a user clicks on the ad. Obviously, an app with loads of intrusive ads is not getting used so they fake the ads and fake the clicks. This makes it appear that you are watching these ads and clicking on the content.The carriers have an incentive to look the other way as they charge you for minutes used. Heavy users will not notice that some percent of their minutes are being used for this scam. Instead they just buy the next level plan to avoid overage fees.
