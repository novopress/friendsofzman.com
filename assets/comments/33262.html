THE SOUL’S DESERT (written August 30, 1939) They are warming up the old horrors; and all that they say is echoes of echoes. Beware of taking sides; only watch. These are not criminals, nor hucksters and little jour- nalists, but the governments Of the great nations; men favorably Representative of massed humanity. Observe them. Wrath and laughter Are quite irrelevant. Clearly it is time To become disillusioned, each person to enter his own soul’s desert And look for God—having seen man. –Robinson JeffersAmerican(1887-1962)
